  • In a post-apocalyptic world, your survival instincts are put to the ultimate test. Welcome to the ruthless battleground of [Islandr.io], a fast-paced 2D shooting game that will push your skills to the limit.

  • Stranded in an abandoned city, you must fend off hordes of enemies while searching for weapons, ammunition, and crucial supplies. As you navigate through desolate streets, haunted buildings, and treacherous landscapes, every step could be your last.

  • But remember, you are not alone. Other survivors, just like you, are fighting for their lives. Will you forge alliances and work together for a greater chance of survival, or will you choose a solitary path, trusting only in your own skills?

  • Upgradable weapons, tactical gear, and power-ups await you at every corner. Equip yourself with an arsenal of devastating firearms to survive. Use W, A, S, D keys to move and use mouse left to shoot (from keyboard), you can change this option from settings.
